# Research Web

Produce a research note with citations and direct links.

Workflow:
1. Break the topic into 2-5 research dimensions.
2. Prioritize official documentation, primary sources, and recent material.
3. Browse broadly first, then open only the most promising sources.
4. Compare sources, note conflicts, and verify version details.
5. Save the result to `./tmp/research/YYYY-MM-DD-web-description.md`.
6. Present the summary, confidence level, and open questions.

Requirements:
- Every factual claim needs a source URL.
- Distinguish official guidance from community opinion.
- Call out software versions, dates, and deprecations.
- Prefer concise synthesis over long quote dumps.
