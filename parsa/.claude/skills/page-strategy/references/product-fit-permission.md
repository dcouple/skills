# Product-Fit Permission

Use this matrix to decide how much product promotion is allowed.

| Product fit | Meaning | Product placement | CTA allowance | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Direct | Product is a native answer to the query | Can lead or be central | Strong | Low |
| Contextual | Product helps after the query is answered | Introduce after useful answer | Moderate | Medium |
| Adjacent | Product relates to a later workflow | Brief mention or related section | Light | High |
| Forced | Product changes the shape of the answer | Avoid or reframe page | None/light | Very high |
| Deceptive | Product is framed as an answer when it is not | Exclude | None | Severe |

## Rules

- Product placement must be earned by fit.
- A CTA is less risky after value than before value.
- Self-ranking a product in a list requires the same criteria applied to every competitor.
- If the brand's product section is much longer than every other option, justify it with reader value or cut it.
- If product copy changes the expected answer set, it is corrupting the page.

## Output Language

Use clear recommendations:

- `Product can lead`
- `Product can appear after the main answer`
- `Product belongs as a related workflow only`
- `Product should be excluded from the core answer`
- `Do not create this page unless the product angle changes`
